Pakistani Omelette

Description

Whip up a spicy and flavorful Pakistani omelette with this easy recipe. Packed with onions, tomatoes, and green chilies, it's perfect for breakfast or brunch!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chopped veggies (onions, tomatoes & green chilli)
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 tbsp Oil

Steps

  1. Cut all veggies
  2. Mix all the seasoning with 1 tbsp of water, set aside
  3. Beaten eggs
  4. Add all the seasoning mixer in beaten eggs
  5. Now add all the veggies and mix it well
  6. Put some oil in pan add all the mixer and cook on medium flame
  7. Toss, when one side done flip it
  8. When another side done, again flip and shake your pan gently (if you did you get even fine color on all over your omelette)
  9. Transfer omelet in your serving plate
  10. Enjoy hot with toasted bread or Lacha Paratha
